Here is a Simple Beginner's list

Preparing for your first camping trip doesn't have to be overwhelming. This simple checklist covers the essentials you will need to stay safe, comfortable, and organised — perfect for beginners who want to enjoy the outdoors without stress.

Shelter

Tent, groundsheet, sleeping bag, sleeping mat

Food & Cooking

Portable stove, fuel, easy meals, water bottle

Safety

First-aid kit, torch, power bank, map

Clothing

Weather-appropriate clothes, jacket, sturdy shoes

Prepare Your Camping Checklist

These checklists will help you pack with confidence. Start with the essentials, add comfort items based on your trip style, and personalise your own list so you are fully prepared for the outdoors.

  • Essential Items

Tips: Focus on safety, warmth, and shelter first. Choose gear suitable for the season and test your equipment at home before heading out.

  • Optional Comfort Items

Tips: These items improve comfort but are not essential. Choose based on trip length, available space, and how much weight you are willing to carry.

  • Personal List

Tips: Use this space for personal needs, campsite rules, medications, documents, or items specific to your destination or group.
